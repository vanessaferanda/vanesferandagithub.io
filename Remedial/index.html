Is self care important?

Self care is important because it helps boost your mental health. Goes the same way with your mental health. Through self care, you can learn about yourselves more. To love and care about yourselves makes your mental health is on check. There's so many ways for you to do self care, you can do some readings, listening to some musics or even just do your own hobby. We made this site to encouraged people to be more aware of their own mental health. We hope through this site, we can be included in your self love journey. Cheers to self love!
